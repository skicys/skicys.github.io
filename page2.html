What's inside a deck
  • Main deck, amount 40-60 cards (Monster cards, Spell cards, Trap cards)
  • Extra deck, maximum 15 cards (Special monster cards)
  • Side deck, maximum 15 cards (Cards that are changeable in a Bo3 competition)
