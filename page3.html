Field zones of Yu-Gi-Oh

Main Monster Zone

Usually, when a monster is Summoned to a Main Monster Zone, its controller chooses the Main Monster Zone to summon it to. For monsters that are Special Summoned to the opposing field by their own summoning condition (such as "Lava Golem"), or an effect that does so (such as "Ojama Trio"), the owner of the card or activator of the effect chooses the zones to summon them to, whereas for an effect that makes both players Special Summon (such as "Crashbug Road"), each player chooses the zones of their own monsters to summon them to.

Spell & Trap Zone

Spell & Trap Zones, are the 5 Zones in which Spell and Trap Cards can be placed on the field (except Field Spell Cards, which are placed in the Field Zone). They are located in the middle of the field behind the Monster Zones; for this reason, they are also sometimes unofficially referred to as "backrow." On game mats, they are often colored cyan.

Field Zone

The Field Zone, is the location Field Spell Cards must be Set and activated (they cannot be Set or activated in a Spell & Trap Zone). It is located in the top left corner of the play mat.

Graveyard

The Graveyard , abbreviated (in English only) as GY in card text from Starter Deck: Link Strike onwards, sometimes translated as Cemetery, and sometimes (unofficially) referred to as the Graveyard Zone or simply Grave, is the name for a player's discard pile.

Extra Deck Zone

The Extra Deck Zone , previously known as the Fusion Deck Zone , is where the Extra Deck (previously known as the Fusion Deck) is placed. It is located in the bottom left corner of the playmat.

Deck Zone

The Deck Zone is where the Main Deck is placed. It is located in the bottom right corner of the play mat.

Extra Monster Zone

Extra Monster Zones are the 2 Monster Zones in the center of the field in which only monsters Summoned from the Extra Deck are placed. These Monster Zones were introduced with the release of Starter Deck 2017 in the OCG and Starter Deck: Link Strike in the TCG.
