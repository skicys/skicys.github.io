Yu-Gi-Oh! Rarity

Guide

Common Rarities:

Common: The most basic card rarity. These cards have no foil or holographic effects, and are typically easy to find.

Rare: These cards have the card name printed in silver foil but otherwise resemble Common cards.

Higher Rarities:

Super Rare: Cards with holographic images but no foil on the card name.

Ultra Rare: Cards with both a holographic image and a gold foil card name.

Secret Rare: These cards feature diagonal, holographic foil lines across the card art, along with a rainbow-colored foil for the card name.

Special and Premium Rarities:

Ultimate Rare: The card’s name and the image both have a textured, raised appearance.

Ghost Rare: The artwork appears faint or ghostly, with a white holographic effect.

Starlight Rare: A very rare form of card with a special foiling that covers the entire card, making it sparkle.

Collector’s Rare: These cards have a subtle holographic effect, mainly focused around the borders and name.
