Normal Summon

Normal Summoning is the most basic form of summoning. You can Normal Summon a monster by placing it in face-up Attack Position.

Special Summon

Special Summoning allows you to bring a monster to the field without using your normal summon. Methods include Fusion, Synchro, and Ritual.

Tribute Summon

Tribute Summoning requires you to sacrifice a monster(s) you control to summon a higher-level monster (Level 5 or higher).

Fusion Summon

Fusion Summoning uses Fusion Materials listed on a Fusion Monster card and requires a spell card like "Polymerization."

Synchro Summon

Synchro Summoning requires a Tuner monster and one or more non-Tuner monsters whose total levels equal the Synchro Monster’s level.

XYZ Summon

XYZ Summoning uses monsters of the same level as materials. The materials are stacked underneath the XYZ monster in the Extra Deck zone.

Link Summon

Link Summoning uses Link Materials equal to the Link Monster’s Link Rating. These monsters do not have levels or defense points.

Pendulum Summon

Pendulum Summoning is unrelated to the effects of the Pendulum Monsters in the Pendulum Zones, and can be performed even if their effects are negated (such as due to the effect of "Vector Pendulum, the Dracoverlord").
